ONE MISSION

Our project is a ML based website where our mission is to provide useful information to Farmers which may
encourage them to grow few commercial crops which is not easily grown in Bihar. Our Website will suggest farmers
that which crop is better option to be grown according to his/her area. Our website will also suggest
the most suitable month to grow specific crops including their traditional method as well as their artificial
methods of plantation. Through our project, we want to contribute to the improvement of
agricultural productivity in the livelihoods of the rural communities in Bihar.
